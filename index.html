Welcome to FitnessMax

FitnessMax is your personal health companion that helps you:

  • Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Estimate your daily calorie needs (TDEE)
  • Choose a fitness goal: lose weight, maintain, or gain muscle
  • Get tailored diet and workout suggestions

Start your journey by heading to the Body Stats Calculator! Then find some suitable workouts you can follow!
